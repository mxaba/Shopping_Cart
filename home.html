Help Guide

Adding item to your order list

To add an item to your order list or cart, simply select the color of your choice: from the color palette, and click the "Add To Cart" Button

Quantity modal

Select the quantity of the color that you would like, you can increament or decrement the quantity you want then, click the Accept Button to add it to your order list

Order details

The details of the order and total price are displayed at the bottom of the page

Removing item from order list

To remove an item from order list under details, simply click the color of the item you wish to remove under Details section.

Checkout

Once you are done with your order list, click the Checkout next to the number displayed, if it's not "Add to Cart, it will be Checkout" Button. A summary of order will be displayed, where you can click "Checkout" Button to complete the order
